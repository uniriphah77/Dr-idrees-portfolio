Patient Care Philosophy

My approach is patient-centered and evidence-based. I begin with thorough assessment and accurate diagnosis, followed by clear communication with patients and families. I individualize management plans using a multidisciplinary approach — during surgery I prioritize safety and function preservation, and postoperatively I focus on monitoring, early complication management, and rehabilitation, while maintaining empathy and professionalism throughout.
